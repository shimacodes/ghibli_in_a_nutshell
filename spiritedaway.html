SUMMARY

A girl accidentally vacations in the wrong dimension, where her parents immediately decide to eat like it's a buffet, and she has to clean up after a bunch of stinky spirits to save them from turning into pork chops forever. Also, there’s a cool dragon-boy with memory issues.

THE GANG

  • Chihiro: a reluctant employee at the world’s weirdest spa
  • Haku: part-time dragon, part-time babysitter.
  • Yubaba: baby-obsessed witch-granny who’ll turn you into a pig if you cross her bathhouse.
