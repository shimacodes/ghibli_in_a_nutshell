ハウルの動く城

SUMMARY

A girl is turned into a grandma for no reason and decides to hitch a ride in a giant magical Roomba owned by a hot but emotionally unstable wizard who’s afraid of commitment. War’s happening, but mostly it’s about Howl’s hair and his bird tantrums.

THE GANG

  • Sophie: the elderly teen trying to make the best of it.
  • Howl: the diva wizard who cares more about his looks than a literal war.
  • Calcifer: a cranky fire-demon with a flair for gossip, who powers a moving castle and complains like it’s a full-time job.
